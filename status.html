Preparation for the Flood

Know Your Risk

Check your flood risk.

Emergency Kit

Food - at least enough for 3 days
Water - one gallon per person per day
Medicine
Batteries
Flashlights
First Aid Kit
Cell phone and charger
Whistle to signal for help

Evacuation Plan

Decide a safe location to meet up with your family members. Check to see if there is a nearby shelter. Get to higher grounds.

During a Flood

Stay Informed

Listen to the news.

Obey Evacuation Orders

If told to evacuate, evacuate immediately. If there is time, disconnect utilities and appliances, move items you want to protect to a higher floor, and put sand bags around your property.

Avoid Flood Water

Do not walk or drive through flood water. There are toxins, chemicals and derbis hidden in the water. Move to a higher ground. If you are trapped call 911 for help.

Disconnect Utilities and Appliances

Safely turn off gas, water, and electricity.

After a Flood

Stay Informed

Listen for updates. Wait until you're told by authorities that everything is all clear to visit disater areas.

Contact Your Family and Loved Ones

Let them know that you are okay.
